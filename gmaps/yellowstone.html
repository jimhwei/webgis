Yellowstone National Park Attractions Map

About

Yellowstone is the first park that started the national parks movement during President Ulysses S. Grant's administration. Known for its stunning landscapes, hot springs and geysers, the park attracts roughly over 4 million visitors in a regular year. The above interactive map is a collection of points of interests when visiting the national park. Click on the icons to see what your next adventure holds.

This project was made possible using bootstrap and JavaScript. The original link came from an unknown user in the Google My Maps community. I was able to get a copy of the GeoJSON file, modify it with ArcGIS Pro and JavaScript back to JSON format for use in this website.
